Strategy 5: Improving connectivity and economic scale

One of the main strategies in the Masterplan for the Acceleration and Expansion of Indonesia’s Economic Development (MP3EI) is to improve the national economic connectivity. The implementation manual outlines the need for integration between logistical and transportation systems and area development plans, among others.

Area connectivity improvement will improve the smoothness of the supply chain and the economic scale of area development activities, allowing economic activities to be implemented more efficiently and effectively. Area connectivity improvement will reduce gaps between commodity production centers and thereby improving the areas’ competitive advantages and economic scale.

Strategy 5 outlines activities needed to improve area connectivity and the economic scale of commodity development. In addition to the economic aspect, connectivity improvement and economic scaling up are also expected to boost green growth and motivate farmers to use sustainable farming practices.

Intervention 5.1. Transportation mode from commodity centers to mills and markets

In the agricultural and plantation sectors in South Sumatra, many production centers of key commodities, including coffee, oil palm and rubber, are not well connected to industrial areas and markets. To improve connectivity and the economic scale of commodity development, the identification of road networks to connect commodity production centers to strategic industrial areas is needed. Building on the identification, a feasibility study should be conducted to take into account the economic and ecological impacts of the construction of roads from production centers to strategic areas. Roads are constructed with financial support from both the government and investors. The road construction is not only prioritized in commodity centers located in strategic areas, but is also planned to reach commodity producers located in inaccessible areas.

Intervensi 5.2. Infrastuktur, tenaga kerja, sarana produksi dan distribusi untuk industri hilir kopi speciality

Industri hilir kopi sangat berpotensi dalam memberikan nilai tambah yang tinggi terutama jika dikembangkan produk diversifikasi produk olahannya dengan rasa khas, seperti kopi speciality Semendo khas Sumatera Selatan. Selain itu, meningkatnya konsumsi kopi dunia menjadi salah satu pendorong bagi industri pengelolahan kopi untuk meningkatkan produksinya. Dalam meningkatkan produksi, industri pengolahan kopi memerlukan suplai bahan baku yang lebih banyak. Oleh karena itu, sebagai bagian dari Strategi 5 untuk meningkatkan skala ekonomi, industri hilir kopi yang ditunjang dengan rantai pasokan beras kopi yang terjamin, infrastruktur yang baik, tenaga kerja yang mencukupi, dan sarana produksi lain yang memadai, antara lain pasokan air bersih dan listrik, merupakan salah satu kunci sukses sektor ini.
